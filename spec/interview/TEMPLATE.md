## Template Usage Notes

### Before the Interview
1. Fill out the participant information and objectives
2. Customize questions based on your research goals
3. Review any materials you'll be sharing with the participant

### During the Interview
1. Take detailed notes in each section
2. Don't be afraid to deviate from the template when interesting topics emerge
3. Capture direct quotes when possible

### After the Interview
1. Complete the analysis section while the conversation is fresh
2. Identify specific action items and timeline
3. Share relevant insights with the team
4. Update the template based on what worked well or could be improved

### Template Customization
- Adjust sections based on your specific research needs
- Add industry-specific questions if targeting particular user segments
- Modify the analysis framework to match your product development process
